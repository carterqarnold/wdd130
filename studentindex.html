Wyoming

Wyoming is a state in western America. Wyoming is known for its cowboys, farms, and ranches. It has a lot of sagebrush and is surrounded by many mountain ranges which are the Rocky Mountains, Wyoming Range, Wind River Range, Teton Range, Absaroka Range, Bighorn Mountains, Salt River Range, Laramie Mountains, and Gros Ventre Range.
